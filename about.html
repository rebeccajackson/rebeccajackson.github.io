About me

Hi, I'm Rebecca

I was born and raised in Zimbabwe, immigrating to New Zealand in 2014.

I have had many jobs, from Safari guide, Canon sales rep to Chef de partie. Each job has been a learning experience and has got me to where I am today.

One of my strengths is my humour, (although not on my Identities and Values list) it is something that keeps me going and I can use it in a positive way to help others. I don't mind making myself look silly in order to make someone smile.
